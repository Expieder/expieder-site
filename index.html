Turning the Real World into an Interactive Canvas

Expieder is an AR-powered social spatial platform where content, movement, and location unlock new ways to connect in the real world.

About Expieder

Expieder is an AR-powered social exploration platform built for the real world.

Users can discover and drop digital content—messages, media, and social profiles—anchored to physical spaces around them.

We’re not waiting for AR glasses to go mainstream—we’re making spatial interaction feel native to the smartphones people already use.

Expieder turns the physical world into a living, interactive canvas, where every place invites connection, creativity, and discovery.

How It Works

Open the app and scan your surroundings. Discover digital pins left by others—notes, videos, or Instagram handles floating in space. Tap to connect, or drop your own and leave a story behind. The world becomes your feed.

Use Cases

Creators: Share exclusive content in specific locations. Gain followers from the real world.

Businesses: Drop live offers and promotions in front of your storefront or event venue.

Explorers: Discover people and stories around you, without needing to follow anyone first.

Why Now?

With 5G rollout, mature AR tools, and billions of smartphones in use, the physical world is ready for a new kind of social interaction. We're not waiting for AR glasses to go mainstream—we're building the interface today.

Our Mission

To turn the physical world into a living, interactive canvas—where every place invites connection, creativity, and discovery. The real world is ready. We're the software catching up to it.
